Love at first site

Once upon a time, in a charming little cafe named mocha, our love story began. It was a sunny day, and our eyes met, sparking an instant connection. From that moment, we knew we were meant to be together. We laughed, shared dreams, and realized it was love at first sight. Our journey of love and adventure began in that cafe six years ago, and we can't wait to celebrate our love with all of you on our special day.

He Proposed

In the very cafe where our love story began, I decided to create another unforgettable chapter. With our favorite place as the backdrop, I got down on one knee and asked you to be my partner for life. The same cafe that witnessed the spark of our love was the perfect place to solidify our commitment. Your 'yes' in that cherished location made it even more special. We can't wait to share the magic of our love story with you on our wedding day.
